# Causal Language Models

Two threads bundled here:

1. **Architecture sense:** decoder-only / autoregressive language models,
   their training dynamics, and behaviors.
2. **Reasoning sense:** using LLMs for causal reasoning, counterfactual
   generation, causal discovery, or tooling around causal-inference
   workflows (e.g. literature review, code generation for `dowhy`/`econml`).

Both threads are in scope; tag whichever is the dominant theme.
