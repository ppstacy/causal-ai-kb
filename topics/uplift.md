# Uplift / Heterogeneous Treatment Effects

Estimating individual-level or subgroup-level treatment effects, and policy
learning over those estimates. Spans meta-learners (T-, S-, X-, R-, DR-),
causal forests, conformal HTE, doubly-robust estimation, and policy
optimization (offline + online).

Most directly relevant to incrementality measurement, targeted marketing,
and any "who responds to the treatment" decision.
