# Topics

Each week's items are tagged with one primary topic. Browse the
intro page for each:

- [Causal Inference]({{ '/topics/causal-inference/' | relative_url }})
  — methodology for estimating causal effects from observational and
  experimental data: CATE, DML, IV, RDD, DiD, synthetic control,
  propensity methods, sensitivity analysis.

- [Uplift / Heterogeneous Treatment Effects]({{ '/topics/uplift/' | relative_url }})
  — individual- or subgroup-level treatment effect estimation and
  policy learning: meta-learners, causal forests, conformal HTE,
  doubly-robust estimation, offline policy optimization.

- [Experimentation]({{ '/topics/experimentation/' | relative_url }})
  — online A/B testing methodology: variance reduction (CUPED, CUPAC),
  sequential testing, interference / SUTVA violations, switchback,
  geo experiments, marketplace experimentation.

- [Causal Language Models]({{ '/topics/causal-lm/' | relative_url }})
  — autoregressive LMs and the use of LLMs for causal reasoning,
  counterfactual generation, and causal-inference tooling.
