# KDD 2025 Workshop — Causal Inference and Machine Learning in Practice

15 accepted papers. Source:
[causal-machine-learning.github.io/kdd2025-workshop](https://causal-machine-learning.github.io/kdd2025-workshop/).

> [!note] Why this is here
> 2025 includes the Netflix/Amazon "Residuals-on-Residuals" paper
> (Winston Chou + Apoorva Lal) and the Thumbtack dynamic-synthetic-controls
> vs panel-aware DML comparison for geo-level marketing impact — both
> directly relevant to incrementality measurement under interference.

## Oral presentations

- **A Debiased Machine Learning Framework for Optimizing Price Promotion within E-commerce** — Bo Zhang, Sergazy Nurbavliyev et al. (Beyond Inc) #dml #pricing
- **An End-to-End Pipeline for Causal ML with Continuous Treatments: An Application to Financial Decision Making** — Javier Moral Hernández, Clara Higuera-Cabañes, Álvaro Ibráin (BBVA) #continuous-treatment #pipeline
- **Auditable Surge Planning: Network-Aware Causal Inference Meets Prescriptive Optimization** — Sanjay Patnala, Vedika Lakhanpal (Georgia Tech) #network #optimization
- **Causal Inference under Threshold Manipulation: A Bayesian Mixture Approach** — Kohsuke Kubota, Shonosuke Sugasawa (NTT DOCOMO / Keio) #bayesian #threshold
- **CIPHER: Causal Intent Plug-in Framework for the Mitigation of Historical Exposure Bias in Recommender Systems** — Sanghyeon Lee et al. (LG Uplus) #recommender #exposure-bias
- **Does Residuals-on-Residuals Regression Produce Representative Estimates of Causal Effects?** — Winston Chou (Netflix), Apoorva Lal (Amazon) #residuals #dml
- **Dynamic Synthetic Controls vs. Panel-Aware Double Machine Learning for Geo-Level Marketing Impact Estimation** — Sang Su Lee, Vineeth Loganathan, Vijay Raghavan (Thumbtack) #synthetic-control #dml #geo
- **I See, Therefore I Do: Estimating Causal Effects for Image Treatments** — Ravi Kolla, Abhinav Thorat, Niranjan Pedanekar (Sony Research India) #image-treatment
- **IMPACT: An Inference-Driven Modeling Framework for Cost-Effective Incentive Allocation in Service Operations** — Yiheng An et al. (Wake Forest / Alibaba) #incentive-allocation
- **Offline Contextual Bandits in the Presence of New Actions** — Yuta Saito, Ren Kishimoto, Tatsuhiro Shimizu, Yusuke Narita et al. (Cornell / Yale / Sony) #bandits #offline
