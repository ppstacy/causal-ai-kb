# KDD 2023 Workshop — Causal Inference and Machine Learning in Practice

11 accepted papers across applied causal-ML in industry. Source:
[causal-machine-learning.github.io/kdd2023-workshop](https://causal-machine-learning.github.io/kdd2023-workshop/).

> [!note] Why this is here
> Each year's CMI workshop is the densest single source of
> applied/industrial causal-ML papers. The accepted papers below are all
> high-signal seeds for [[uplift]], [[causal-inference]], and
> [[experimentation]].

## Oral presentations

- **Leveraging Causal Uplift Modeling for Budget Constrained Benefits Allocation** — Dmitri Goldenberg, Javier Albert (Booking.com) #uplift #budget-constrained
- **Ensemble Method for Estimating Individualized Treatment Effects** — Kevin Wu Han, Han Wu (Stanford) #ite #ensemble
- **A Scalable and Debiased Approach to Dynamic Pricing with Causal Machine Learning and Optimization** — Nicolò Cosimo Albanese, Fabian Furrer, Marco Guerriero (AWS) #dml #pricing
- **An IPW-based Unbiased Ranking Metric in Two-sided Markets** — Keisho Oh, Naoki Nishimura et al. (Recruit) #ipw #ranking
- **Power and Pre-treatment Fit: Optimizing Synthetic Control Method for Quasi-experiments** — Ali O Polat (Shipt) #synthetic-control #power
- **Dynamic Causal Structure Discovery and Causal Effect Estimation** — Jianian Wang, Rui Song (NC State) #causal-discovery #effect-estimation
- **Hierarchical Clustering As a Novel Solution to the Notorious Multicollinearity Problem in Observational Causal Inference** — Yufei Wu, Zhiying Gu, Alex Deng, Jacob Zhu (Airbnb) #observational #multicollinearity

## Poster presentations

- **Community Detection-Enhanced Causal Structural Learning** — Yuhe Gao, Hengrui Cai, Sheng Zhang, Rui Song #causal-discovery
- **ACE: Active Learning for Causal Inference with Expensive Experiments** — Difan Song, Simon Mak, C.F. Jeff Wu #active-learning
- **Machine Learning based Framework for Robust Price-Sensitivity Estimation with Application to Airline Pricing** — Shahin Boluki, Ravi Kumar (PROS) #pricing #airline
- **OpportunityFinder: A Framework for Automated Causal Inference** — Huy Nguyen, Prince Grover, Devashish Khatwani (Amazon) #automl #causal
