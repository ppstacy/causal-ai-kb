# KDD 2024 Workshop — Causal Inference and Machine Learning in Practice

12 accepted papers. Source:
[causal-machine-learning.github.io/kdd2024-workshop](https://causal-machine-learning.github.io/kdd2024-workshop/).

> [!note] Why this is here
> 2024 had a strong applied-CI lineup, including a sensitivity-analysis
> use case at Booking.com co-authored by Chernozhukov and Cinelli — the
> canonical sensitivity-analysis citation in production form.

## Oral presentations

- **From Causality to Incrementality: Estimating Marketplace Inter-product Cannibalisation Effects at Expedia Group** — Florian Florian, Catriona Mitchison (Expedia) #cannibalisation #marketplace
- **DISCO: constrained bandits for personalized discount allocation within fashion e-commerce** — Jason Zhang, Benjamin M Howson, Panayiota Savva, Eleanor Loh (TripAdvisor / Imperial / ASOS) #bandits #discount
- **Industrial-Grade Smart Troubleshooting through Causal Technical Language Processing** — Alexandre Trilla, Ossee Yiboe, Nenad Mijatovic, Jordi Vitria (Alstom / Universitat de Barcelona) #causal-lm #nlp
- **Practical Marketplace Optimization at Uber Using Causally-Informed Machine Learning** — Zijun Qin et al. (Uber) #marketplace #causal-ml
- **Enhancing Uplift Modeling in Multi-Treatment Marketing Campaigns: Leveraging Score Ranking and Calibration Techniques** — Yoon Tae Park, Ting Xu (Best Buy) #uplift #calibration
- **Optimization and Control Applications through Repeated Interventions Based on Structural Causal Models** — Daigo Fujiwara, Tomonori Izumitani, Shohei Shimizu (NTT / Shiga / RIKEN) #scm #control
- **Understanding Product Quality with Unstructured Data: An Application of LLMs and Embeddings at Airbnb** — Zhiying Gu, Jacob Zhu, Brad Li, Linsha Chen (Airbnb) #llm #embeddings
- **Sensitivity Analysis for Causal ML: A Use Case at Booking.com** — Philipp Bach, Victor Chernozhukov, Carlos Cinelli, Lin Jia, Sven Klaassen, Nils Skotara, Martin Spindler (Hamburg / MIT / UWashington / Booking.com) #sensitivity-analysis #booking
