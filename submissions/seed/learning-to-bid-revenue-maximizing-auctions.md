## Summary

Online learning approach to bidding in repeated auctions where the
seller's reserve / mechanism changes over time. Develops a no-regret
bidding strategy under a strategic-buyer setting with revenue-maximizing
sellers.

## Why relevant

> [!note] Why this is here
> Auction-design and bidding mechanics sit alongside causal measurement
> in ad systems. Worth keeping as a reference for any work that combines
> bidding-policy learning with incrementality measurement (e.g. when bid
> policy itself becomes the treatment whose effect is estimated).
