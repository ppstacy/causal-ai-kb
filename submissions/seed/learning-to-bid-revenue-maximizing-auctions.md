# Learning to bid in revenue-maximizing auctions

- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/1902.10427

## Summary

Online learning approach to bidding in repeated auctions where the
seller's reserve / mechanism changes over time. Develops a no-regret
bidding strategy under a strategic-buyer setting with revenue-maximizing
sellers.

## Why relevant

Auction-design and bidding mechanics sit alongside causal measurement in
ad systems. Worth keeping in the KB as a reference for any work that
combines bidding-policy learning with incrementality measurement (e.g.
when bid policy itself becomes the treatment whose effect is estimated).

## Keywords

auction theory, revenue maximization, real-time bidding, online learning,
no-regret algorithms, bid optimization, strategic agents, mechanism design
